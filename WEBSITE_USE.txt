================================================================================
                    CleanMyCity - Website Purpose & How to Use
================================================================================

CleanMyCity is an interactive web platform designed to keep our urban spaces clean
by combining artificial intelligence (AI), mapping technology, and gamification.

The website serves two primary purposes: empowering citizens to clean their 
neighborhoods, and assisting city administrators in managing waste collection.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. FOR CITIZENS: Earn Rewards for Keeping the City Clean
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Every day, we dispose of items. CleanMyCity turns proper waste disposal into a
rewarding, game-like experience:

* Sync & Login: 
  Enter your username to access your citizen profile. As you dispose of waste,
  you'll earn Eco Balance Points (XP) and unlock badges (like "Green Novice").

* Dispose and Earn XP:
  Take a photo of your trash being discarded in the correct container.
  - Recyclables (e.g., plastic bottles, aluminum cans): Earn 50 XP.
  - General Waste (e.g., non-recyclable trash): Earn 20 XP.
  - Littered items (e.g., reporting garbage piles on the street): Earn 0 XP for
    disposal, but logs a location report for cleanup.

* AI Camera Verification:
  Our built-in AI checks your photo in real-time. It ensures:
  1. The photo is not a duplicate (checks for spam).
  2. The trash bin or item is properly centered and visible in the photo.
  3. The item is correctly identified as recyclable or non-recyclable.

* Interactive Waste Map:
  Use the map to spot active trash containers, recycling hubs, and littered 
  streets in your neighborhood.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. FOR CITY OPERATIONS & SANITATION TEAMS (Ops Dashboard)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Municipal authorities and sanitation squads use the dashboard side of the website
to coordinate cleanup tasks:

* Real-Time Incident Stream:
  Whenever a citizen uploads a photo of a littered street, the report pops up on
  the operations feed instantly using a live Server-Sent Events (SSE) feed.

* Garbage Heatmap:
  Administrators can look at the map to see exactly where litter reports are 
  concentrated. This helps city councils decide where to place more trash cans
  and dispatch street sweepers.

* Smart Resource Dispatch:
  Sanitation teams can view active coordinates of trash bins and littered locations
  to plan their collection routes efficiently, saving fuel and time.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Summary of the Flow:
Citizen spots litter/disposes waste -> Takes a photo -> AI validates -> Map is
updated -> Sanitation crew cleans up -> Citizen earns XP!
================================================================================
